125 166 The Ponspectors are an elite band of surveyors from Frontier Village, split between red, blue and yellow teams. If you can [System:Color name=arts_sp ]befriend members from all three teams[/System:Color], Shulk and his friends will [System:Color name=arts_sp ]gain access to a new kind of special attack[/System:Color], so try to seek them out wherever possible!
126 166 The full Ponspector brigade numbers [System:Color name=arts_sp ]12 members[/System:Color]. When you recruit one, they may tip you off to [System:Color name=arts_sp ]the location of others[/System:Color]. Locations you've heard of are marked on the map with a [ML:icon icon=map01 ].
127 166 Ponspectors on the Bionis' Shoulder have [System:Color name=arts_sp ]issues [/System:Color]they struggle with. Resolve those issues for them to gain their help in your quest.
128 166 In the Collectables section of the main menu, you can access the [System:Color name=arts_sp ]Ponspector Report [/System:Color]to learn more about the Ponspectors you have befriended up until that point.

130 29 Ponspector assistance
131 29 High Ponspector tension 1
132 29 High Ponspector tension 2
133 166 In battle against enemies, Ponspectors will jump in to help Shulk and his friends on their own initiative. Each Ponspector has a [System:Color name=arts_sp ]signature move[/System:Color], healing the party, debuffing the enemies, and so on.
134 166 If you perform well in Battle Start Affinity, Burst Affinity, etc. by pressing [ML:icon icon=btn_b ] at the right time, the Ponspectors may enter a state of [System:Color name=arts_sp ]High Tension[/System:Color].
135 166 Ponspectors in a state of [System:Color name=arts_sp ]High Tension [/System:Color]will join in the battle with all their strength, and with no slacking off. High Tension will wear off with time, returning to the usual state.

155 166 Fogbeasts are more powerful than their regular monster equivalents. In addition to that, they also spread a [System:Color name=arts_sp ]riot surge [/System:Color]in a set radius around themselves, drawing in nearby monsters.

Additionally, these enemies will be affected by the [System:Color name=arts_sp ]Riot [/System:Color]Aura ([ML:icon icon=enh35 ]), [System:Color name=arts_sp ]increasing their level [/System:Color]based on the strength of the Fogbeast.

200 15 Expert Mode Tips
201 29 Adjusting levels 1
202 29 Adjusting levels 2

204 166 The [System:Color name=arts_sp ]Expert Mode[/System:Color] section of the System menu ([ML:icon icon=btn_plus ]) lets you manually adjust party characters' levels. Increasing a character's level will cost reserve EXP. Reducing a character's level will convert excess experience points back into reserve EXP.
205 166 Level adjustment is performed individually for each character. Select your desired character with [ML:icon icon=btn_a ], then pick the desired level using [ML:icon icon=btn_left ] and [ML:icon icon=btn_right ], and then confirm it with [ML:icon icon=btn_a ].
